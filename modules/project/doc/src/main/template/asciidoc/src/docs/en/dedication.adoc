= Dedication

This book is dedicated to the Mifos open-source community, the countless contributors across every time zone who donate their evenings, weekends, and coffee breaks to making financial inclusion a reality for the two billion people the world has left behind.

Every line of code you write, every bug you triage, every translation you submit matters.  You are the reason a community bank in rural Kenya can process a loan in seconds, and a microfinance institution in the Philippines can serve ten times the clients it once could.

To you, dear contributor, for showing up again and again.

Thank you.
